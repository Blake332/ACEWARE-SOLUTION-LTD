About Us

ACEWARE SOLUTION LIMITED is a Kenyan-based supply, logistics, and technology solutions company dedicated to supporting businesses, institutions, and organizations with reliable products and efficient service delivery.

Who We Are

We are a professional supplier of computer accessories, stationery, printing consumables, media accessories, and logistics services. Our operations are designed to meet the needs of corporate clients, NGOs, schools, and government institutions.

Our Expertise

With a strong supplier network and efficient logistics coordination, we ensure consistent availability, quality assurance, and timely delivery of all supplied products.

Our Coverage

We serve clients across Kenya and East Africa, offering both scheduled and on-demand delivery supported by dependable transport and logistics systems.
